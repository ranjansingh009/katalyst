About Us

Vaidic Guide - Manish Jayswal

Stars have always intrigued me. As a child I would gaze upon them, endlessly, wondering what secrets they hide. I finished school, university and then travelled around the world as a financial risk professional, not knowing what my destiny is. When i was a teenager, I stumbled upon Astrology out of academic interest didn't take it seriously and considered it as a source of entertainment.

I had several setbacks in my life, losing my mother, losing my job and chronic health condition all at the same time. The challenges that i faced pushed me to question "Why me?", "What have i done to deserve this?" As an incorrigible optimist and someone who likes to find deeper reasons in each and every action, i turned to astrology.

Although, i have been learning astrology for the past several years, 2020 was a defining year for me. Under the guidance of a renowned Vaidic Astrologer from India, i had the opportunity to experience wonders of the Vaidic Astrology.

Astrology is not just about predicting the future, it is a window into your life, your soul, but most of all your universe. You are born at a particular time to experience joys of the universe, experience learning, accumulate positive karmas and pass on the knowledge. Vaidic astrology will give you the opportunity to understand and fulfil the reasons of your existence. It will give you a bird's eye view of your journey thus far and at the same time delve into micro details enabling you to decide on your next steps. You are the master of your destiny, Michelangelo of your life, let Vaidic Astrology be the brush to paint that masterpiece with.

I am passionate about helping people and nothing pleases more to guide people through uncertainty. So if you are facing a dilemma, facing uncertainty associated with relationships, money, career, health or any other matter that is inexplicable, Vaidic Astrology will offer you an explanation and solution to manage uncertainty.

I will never claim that I will predict your future. I do, however, claim that a session with me will be the catalyst you need to help create a future that you deserve.

Vaidic Guide - Ujjwal Mathur

Of Numbers, Stocks, and Astrology- The Logic of my Stars?

Well, curious keywords such as these will lead you somewhere only if you choose to go down the rabbit hole. For me, the journey began thus. I developed an interest in astrology in my formative years, much earlier than my peers. I lived in awe of the accuracy of the predictions, of the past and the future. A focus on the immediate distracted me from the larger picture that emerges in the template of the universe, derived from astrology.

We are generally overwhelmed by what astrologers tell us, such that we end up having closed answers to closed questions like: will I find true love? Or when and who will I marry? Which career would make me most money? The list is indeed endless. But I have always viewed astrology to be larger than life itself, rather as a road that leads to contentment, happiness, and serenity.

I believe it is a journey to identify and nurture one's untapped wisdom and potential. I decided to pursue the practice of astrology few years ago. To my surprise when I started reading & comprehending the magic of the stars (experimenting with my horoscope), I discovered an interesting trend weaved through the fabric of my timeline/life: I learnt that I was meant to make a difference in this world through my knowledge and expertise in 'Numbers'. To state an anecdote from my life, I have only worked nine years throughout my life, and investing using my craft of numbers has been my mainstay since. Imagine what I can do for you!

So here I am, to fulfil my destiny and help you connect the dots of your life so you could have a more mindful & learned approach towards the decisions that will shape your future.
